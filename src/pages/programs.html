Our Programs

Designed around dignity, empowerment, and opportunity.

Through community-driven initiatives, Tunza Dada supports girls, women, and families with programs that create lasting impact across education, health, leadership, and economic empowerment.

Menstrual Health Support

Providing sanitary products, menstrual health education, and safe spaces that enable girls to stay in school confidently and with dignity.

Education & Mentorship

Supporting girls through mentorship, academic guidance, life skills training, and access to educational resources.

Economic Empowerment

Equipping women with business skills, financial literacy, and opportunities to improve household income.

Leadership Development

Building confident young leaders who can influence positive change within their communities.

Community Outreach

Working directly with communities to address challenges, promote awareness, and strengthen local support systems.

Advocacy & Awareness

Championing gender equality, education access, and health rights through campaigns and partnerships.

Tunza Dada

Empowering girls, women, and families with dignified programs in Nairobi, Kenya. Every shilling goes directly to the community we serve.
