Our Programs

Designed around dignity, safety, and opportunity.

Menstrual Health Support

We provide safe menstrual products, health education, and supportive spaces so girls can attend school with confidence.

Learning & Mentorship

Scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship keep girls engaged in education and on track for future success.

Women’s Livelihoods

Business training, savings groups, and income opportunities help women build stable futures for their families.

Community Leadership

Local leaders receive coaching and support to sustain programs and amplify youth voices in decision-making.

Emergency Response

When families face crisis, we respond with care packages, shelter assistance, and rapid support networks.

Advocacy & Awareness

We raise awareness about gender equity, health rights, and education access through campaigns and partner events.
